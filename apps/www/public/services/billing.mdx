<H2>Cycle</H2>


<C>
The billing cycle operates on a weekly basis, where charges are made for closed tasks from the preceding week.
</C>



<C>
In the event that a client fails to remit payment within the agreed-upon timeframe, then all ongoing development activities will be paused for that client until payment is received.
</C>




<H2 id="task-cost">
Task Cost
</H2>


<C>
Each task comes with a predefined estimate, typically ranging from five minutes to an hour, which remains fixed. In the event of full-scale <L href="/services/full-software-solution">project development</L>, it often involves more than just myself. Therefore, each <L href="/services/glossary#team">team</L> member has their own hourly rate, which will be <L href="/services/communication">communicated</L> to you. Where the cost of each task is determined by the hourly rate of the closer times the amount of time in hours it took to close.

</C>
